Memorial Day 2026: What's Open, What's Closed, and How Americans Are Celebrating

📅 2026-05-25 ⏱️ 3 min read 📝 727 words

Memorial Day 2026 falls on Monday, May 25th, and it marks more than just the unofficial start of summer. It's a day to honor the men and women who gave their lives serving in the United States military. Whether you're planning a backyard barbecue, heading to a parade, or taking advantage of holiday sales, here's everything you need to know about Memorial Day this year.

What's the Meaning Behind Memorial Day?

Memorial Day is not the same as Veterans Day. Veterans Day (November 11) honors all military veterans. Memorial Day specifically honors those who died while serving. It originated after the Civil War and became a federal holiday in 1971.

The National Moment of Remembrance asks all Americans to pause at 3:00 PM local time for one minute of silence. Many communities hold wreath-laying ceremonies, parades, and flag-lowering ceremonies at cemeteries.

What's Open on Memorial Day 2026?

Open (most locations):

  • Grocery stores: Walmart, Target, Kroger, Costco (may have reduced hours)
  • Retail: Most malls and shopping centers are open with Memorial Day sales
  • Restaurants: Most restaurants are open, many with special brunch menus
  • Home improvement: Home Depot, Lowe's — big sales on outdoor equipment
  • Gas stations, pharmacies, convenience stores

Closed on Memorial Day 2026:

  • Banks and financial markets: Stock exchanges are closed
  • Federal offices: Post offices, DMV, government buildings
  • Schools and universities
  • Most corporate offices
💡 Pro Tip: No mail delivery on Memorial Day. If you're expecting a package through USPS, it'll arrive Tuesday. UPS and FedEx also have modified schedules — check their websites for your area.
