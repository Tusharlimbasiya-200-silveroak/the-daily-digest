Air Fryer Recipes for Beginners: 5 Things to Make First

📅 2026-05-25 ⏱️ 3 min read 📝 601 words

You bought an air fryer because everyone said it would change your life. Now it's sitting on your counter and you're not sure what to do with it besides make frozen fries. Let's fix that. These 5 recipes showcase what an air fryer does best — and they're all dead simple for first-timers.

1. Crispy Chicken Wings (Best Introduction)

This is what your air fryer was born to do.

  • 2 lbs chicken wings, pat dry
  • 1 tbsp baking powder (the secret to crispiness without deep frying)
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder

Instructions: Toss wings with baking powder and seasonings. Air fry at 380°F for 20 minutes, flip, then 400°F for 5 more minutes. Toss in your favorite sauce (buffalo, BBQ, honey garlic).

The result: crispy skin, juicy meat, zero deep frying oil. This is the recipe that makes people fall in love with their air fryer.

2. Air Fryer Salmon (12 minutes)

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • Olive oil spray
  • Salt, pepper, lemon juice, garlic powder

Instructions: Season salmon, place in air fryer skin-side down. 400°F for 8-10 minutes. No flipping needed. The outside gets a light crust while the inside stays moist and flaky.

💡 Pro Tip: Don't overcrowd your air fryer basket. Air needs to circulate around the food to make it crispy. If items are piled on top of each other, you'll get steamed food instead of crispy food. Cook in batches if necessary.
