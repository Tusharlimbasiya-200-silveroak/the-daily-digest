How to Choose the Right TV Size: A Simple Guide

📅 2026-05-25 ⏱️ 2 min read 📝 465 words

You know that feeling when you buy a TV and it looks huge in the store but tiny on your wall at home? Or worse, you get one that's so big it gives you a headache? Choosing the right TV size isn't about budget — it's about your room. Here's a simple formula that gets it right every time.

The Simple Formula

Measure the distance from your couch to where your TV will sit (in inches). Divide by 1.5. That's your ideal TV size.

  • 6 feet away (72"): 48-inch TV
  • 8 feet away (96"): 65-inch TV
  • 10 feet away (120"): 75-85-inch TV
  • 12 feet away (144"): 85-98-inch TV

Most people sit 8-10 feet from their TV, which means 65-75 inches is ideal for most living rooms.

💡 Pro Tip: When in doubt, go bigger. Almost everyone who buys a TV says they wish they'd gone one size up. You adjust to a big TV quickly, but a too-small TV is frustrating forever.

Room-by-Room Recommendations

  • Bedroom (5-7 ft viewing distance): 43-55 inches
  • Living room (8-10 ft): 65-75 inches
  • Large living room (10-12 ft): 75-85 inches
  • Home theater/basement: 85-98 inches or a projector
  • Kitchen/office: 32-43 inches

What About 4K vs 1080p?

At 65 inches and above, get 4K. You'll see the difference. At 43 inches and below, 1080p is fine — you'd need to press your face against the screen to notice the difference. Between 43-65 inches, 4K is preferred but not critical.

📌 Real-Life Example: Jeff upgraded from a 55-inch TV to a 75-inch TV in a living room with a 10-foot viewing distance. "The 55 felt like I was squinting at a monitor. The 75-inch fills my vision perfectly — it's like having a movie theater at home. I can't believe I waited so long to upgrade."

Best TVs by Size in 2026

  • 43-50 inch: TCL S5 ($200-300) — best budget, great picture quality
  • 55 inch: Hisense U7 ($400) — excellent mid-range with great brightness
  • 65 inch: Sony Bravia 7 ($1,000) or LG C4 OLED ($1,300) — premium picture
  • 75 inch: Samsung QN85D ($1,200) — big screen without OLED price
  • 85 inch: TCL QM7 ($1,500) — enormous screen, surprisingly affordable
🎯 Key Takeaway: Use the formula: viewing distance in inches ÷ 1.5 = ideal TV size. For most living rooms (8-10 feet away), a 65-75 inch TV is perfect. Always go bigger if you're torn between two sizes — you'll never regret it. The TCL and Hisense brands offer incredible value if you're on a budget.
