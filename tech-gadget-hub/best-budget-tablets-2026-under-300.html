Best Budget Tablets 2026: Great Tablets Under $300

📅 2026-05-25 ⏱️ 3 min read 📝 514 words

Not everyone needs a $1,000 iPad Pro. If you want a tablet for streaming Netflix on the couch, reading books, browsing the web, or keeping kids entertained, you can get an excellent one for under $300. Here are the best budget tablets in 2026.

Best Overall: iPad 10th Gen ($279)

Apple's entry-level iPad is hard to beat. It has a gorgeous 10.9-inch display, runs the full iPad app library, and lasts 10 hours on a single charge. It handles streaming, web browsing, note-taking, and casual gaming without breaking a sweat.

The iPad app ecosystem is its biggest advantage — almost every app is optimized for the iPad's screen. Android tablets still can't match this level of app quality.

Best for Samsung Users: Samsung Galaxy Tab A9+ ($199)

Samsung's budget tablet offers a big 11-inch display, expandable storage (up to 1TB with a microSD card), and Samsung DeX for a desktop-like experience. If you have a Samsung phone, the integration is seamless.

💡 Pro Tip: If you're choosing between the iPad and Samsung tablet, ask yourself: do I need expandable storage? The iPad starts at 64GB with no expansion. The Samsung starts at 64GB but you can add a $15 microSD card for 256GB more.

Best for Kids: Amazon Fire HD 10 ($139)

Amazon's Fire tablets are practically indestructible, and at $139, you won't cry if your toddler drops it. Amazon Kids+ ($5/month) gives unlimited access to thousands of kid-friendly books, games, videos, and educational apps.

The parental controls are the best of any tablet — set screen time limits, bedtimes, and content filters. You can even make kids complete educational goals before accessing games.

📌 Real-Life Example: The Martinez family bought two Fire HD 10 tablets for their kids (ages 5 and 8). "We set a rule: 30 minutes of reading before games unlock. The kids actually read more now because they're motivated. Best $280 we ever spent."

Best for Reading: Amazon Kindle Scribe ($239)

If your primary use is reading and note-taking, the Kindle Scribe is fantastic. The e-ink display is easy on the eyes — no blue light, no glare, readable in direct sunlight. You can handwrite notes directly on books and documents with the included stylus.

Best Value: Lenovo Tab M11 ($179)

Lenovo's Tab M11 punches above its weight with an 11-inch 90Hz display, quad speakers, and included pen. It's great for note-taking, drawing, and entertainment. At $179, it's the best value Android tablet you can get.

🎯 Key Takeaway: The iPad 10th Gen ($279) is the best budget tablet overall — nothing beats its app ecosystem. For kids, the Amazon Fire HD 10 ($139) with Amazon Kids+ is unbeatable. For reading, the Kindle Scribe ($239) is in a class of its own. You don't need to spend $500+ to get a great tablet experience in 2026.
