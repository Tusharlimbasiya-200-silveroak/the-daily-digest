Healthy Snacks That Actually Taste Good: 15 Ideas Under 200 Calories

📅 2026-05-25 ⏱️ 3 min read 📝 632 words

The word "healthy snack" usually conjures images of sad celery sticks and dry rice cakes. No wonder people reach for chips instead. But healthy snacks can absolutely taste great — you just need better options. These 15 snacks are all under 200 calories, genuinely satisfying, and won't make you feel like you're on a punishment diet.

Sweet Cravings

1. Frozen Grapes (60 cal per cup)

Wash grapes, throw them in the freezer for 2 hours. They taste like candy — sweet, refreshing, and satisfying. Kids love them too.

2. Greek Yogurt with Honey (150 cal)

A cup of plain Greek yogurt with a drizzle of honey and a handful of berries. High protein keeps you full for hours.

3. Dark Chocolate Square + Almonds (180 cal)

2 squares of dark chocolate (70%+) and 8-10 almonds. The combination of sweet, rich, and crunchy satisfies every craving.

4. Apple Slices with Cinnamon (95 cal)

Slice an apple and sprinkle with cinnamon. The cinnamon adds warmth and sweetness without any sugar. Microwave for 30 seconds and it tastes like apple pie filling.

5. Banana Ice Cream (105 cal)

Freeze a ripe banana. Blend until creamy. That's it — it becomes genuinely ice cream-like in texture. Add cocoa powder for chocolate "ice cream."

Salty/Savory Cravings

6. Everything Bagel Seasoned Avocado (120 cal)

Half an avocado sprinkled with Everything Bagel seasoning. Eat with a spoon. Sounds weird, tastes incredible.

7. Air-Popped Popcorn (93 cal per 3 cups)

Three full cups of popcorn for under 100 calories. Add a light spray of olive oil and salt. Way more satisfying than a handful of chips.

💡 Pro Tip: Pre-portion snacks into individual bags or containers on Sunday. When hunger hits, you grab a pre-measured amount instead of eating straight from the bag (which always leads to eating 3x more than intended).
